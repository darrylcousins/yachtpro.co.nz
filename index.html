YACHTPRO - Worldwide Yacht Delivery

Yachtpro is a small, professional and personal New Zealand (NZ) based worldwide yacht delivery service that understands offshore yachts (sailboats) and their delivery requirements. Don Dick has been a delivery skipper for 20yrs and his partner, Sally Forde, is a registered nurse. Together they have sailed over 350 000 nautical miles (nm). Don has been skipper/navigator for those offshore (bluewater) miles which were sailed on both monohulls and catamarans. They have sailed most oceans and transited the Panama Canal many times whilst on delivery.

We take pride in our conservative and efficient sailing style.

We have a clean insurance record and hold NZ passports with US visas. Both of us maintain good health and are non smokers. Our independent navigation systems, paper charts and satellite phone are part of our delivery equipment.

Pricing varies according to vessel, length and condition. Short deliveries are by nautical mile. Longer deliveries can be negotiated on a weekly basis or by mile. Safety standards for delivery vessels must be similar to NZ Category One.

Our fees are very competitive, you get a skipper and crew for the price of one!

References are available on request.

Please call, we are happy to share our knowledge if you are contemplating offshore purchase or wanting your boat delivered.

Safe sailing.

Don and Sally.
